The Process

What to Expect

Every engagement follows the same principles, regardless of scope:

  • Discovery first. We start with a conversation — no commitment, no pressure. I need to understand your situation before proposing anything.
  • Diagnosis before prescription. I document what I find, share it with you, and we agree on priorities together. No surprises.
  • Build with you, not for you. Your team stays involved. My job is to build capacity, not dependency.
  • Transparent progress. You see what I see. Regular check-ins, shared documentation, no black boxes.
  • Knowledge transfer. When the engagement ends, you own everything. The systems, the documentation, the knowledge. It’s yours.

FAQ

Common Questions

How much does consulting cost?

Pricing depends on scope. The AI Workflow Audit (2–3 weeks) is the lightest engagement. Custom Agent Build (4–6 weeks) and AI Team Architecture (8–12 weeks) scale from there. Every engagement starts with a free discovery call where I learn your situation and recommend the right scope — or tell you if consulting isn’t what you need.

What industries do you work with?

50+ teams across marketing, operations, financial services, real estate, healthcare, robotics, media, and professional services. The diagnostic frameworks are industry-agnostic — because the underlying problems are the same everywhere: smart people, disconnected systems, wasted capacity. Daniel adapts the process to your context, not the other way around.

Do you work remotely or only in Birmingham?

Both. Daniel is based in Birmingham, AL and works with local clients in person, but most engagements are remote. The AI systems, documentation, and training are all delivered digitally. Discovery calls and check-ins happen over video.

What happens on a discovery call?

A 30-minute conversation where Daniel learns about your business, your current technology stack, and what’s not working. No pitch, no slides, no pressure. If there’s a fit, he’ll recommend a specific engagement. If there isn’t, he’ll tell you — and often make an introduction to someone who can help.

What if AI isn’t right for my business?

That’s a real possibility, and Daniel will tell you. The AI Workflow Audit exists specifically to answer this question. Not every process benefits from AI — sometimes the issue is people or process, not technology. The audit identifies where AI fits and where it doesn’t, so you invest in the right place.

Do I own the systems you build?

Yes — everything. When the engagement ends, you own the agents, the documentation, the knowledge bases, and the training materials. DDV builds capacity, not dependency. Your team will know how to maintain and evolve the system without Daniel.

Ready to make your systems intentional?

No pitch — just a conversation about where you are, where you want to be, and whether I can help.

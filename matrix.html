The AI Opportunity Matrix

Find exactly where AI fits YOUR work — in 30 minutes. No expertise required. No jargon. Just a straightforward exercise that works for any profession.

What You’ll Walk Away With

You’ve tried AI. Maybe ChatGPT, maybe Claude, maybe whatever your company bought a license for. And sometimes it’s great. But most of the time? It’s … fine. Generic. Not worth the effort of explaining what you need.

Here’s what nobody told you: the issue isn’t your prompts. It’s that you haven’t thought about where AI actually fits your work.

The AI Opportunity Matrix fixes that. In about 30 minutes, you’ll map your real work tasks into three clear categories using a simple traffic-light system:

●
GREEN — AI Accelerates
Use AI confidently. Give direction, review the output.
●
YELLOW — AI Assists
Use AI carefully. Verify everything before it ships.
●
RED — AI Avoids
Keep AI out entirely. Handle these yourself.

The key insight: your thinking is the constant. AI changes what you do. It doesn’t change that you need to think.

Three directions for paytonjunkin.com

Three home page concepts built against the Vibe & Reference Pack and the JC2026 Style Guide. All three follow the same brand law — ivory, navy, light blue, photography-led, no popups, no urgency, mobile-first. They differ in personality, density, and where the search bar lives.

The question to answer when comparing them isn't "which is prettier" — all three can be made beautiful. The question is which one is yours: which feels like the way you'd talk about Birmingham real estate to someone you respect, on a Tuesday, over coffee.

If a section in any of these doesn't feel right, that's good information. Open them on your phone too — mobile is where 60-70% of these will get viewed.

Direction one

Search-Forward

Modern House · Redfin

The most utility-first of the three. Hero is full-bleed photography with a dominant search bar. Listings carousel and neighborhood tiles below.

  • Search bar dominates above the fold
  • "I'll help you find it"
  • Best for buyer-search-intent traffic
